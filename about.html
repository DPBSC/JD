About

Built on craft and care.

BuildScape Electric is a New York–based electrical contractor delivering high-quality work across residential, commercial, and creative spaces. Founded in 2021, the company is NYC licensed and built on over 20 years of combined industry experience.

The team specializes in thoughtful, detail-driven electrical solutions—from full-scale installations and renovations to lighting design and system upgrades. Known for reliability, clear communication, and clean execution, BuildScape Electric approaches every project with a focus on safety, efficiency, and long-term performance.

Serving clients across New York City, BuildScape partners with homeowners, designers, and businesses to bring projects to life with precision and care.
