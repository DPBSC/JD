Services

What we do.

A full-scope electrical practice serving residential, commercial, and creative spaces across New York City.

  • Residential

    Wiring, outlets, fixtures, lighting, and panel work for apartments, lofts, and brownstones. Clean finishes that respect existing interiors and meet NYC code.

  • Retail & Commercial Projects

    Storefronts, restaurants, offices, and showrooms. Full electrical buildouts coordinated with GCs, designers, and store planners to keep openings on schedule.

  • New Construction & Rehab

    Complete electrical scope for new builds and gut rehabs—from rough-in and panel sizing through finish trim, inspections, and final walkthrough.

  • Lighting Design

    Recessed, track, accent, decorative, and architectural lighting. Layered plans built around how a space is actually used, with dimming and zoning that feel intuitive.

  • Panel & Service Upgrades

    Service increases, panel replacements, sub-panels, EV chargers, and code corrections. Clear scope, permit handling, and clean transitions back to power.

  • Smart Home & Automation

    Lutron lighting, smart switches, motorized shades, and integrations with home hubs. Reliable systems that stay simple for the people who live with them.

  • Intercom Systems

    Modern intercom installs and replacements for multi-unit buildings, lofts, and offices. Audio, video, and IP-based systems with mobile app access.

  • Audio Visual

    TV mounting, in-wall and in-ceiling speakers, projector installs, conference room AV, and the low-voltage cabling behind it. Concealed runs, finished result.

  • Security Systems

    Cameras, access control, alarm systems, and motion sensors. Designed for clear coverage and easy day-to-day use, integrated with existing networks.

  • Repairs & Diagnostics

    Troubleshooting flickering circuits, dead outlets, code violations, and emergency calls. Fast response with a focus on root cause, not band-aids.
